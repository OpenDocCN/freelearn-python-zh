Part 2: Deep Dive – Building Blocks of Metaprogramming I

The objective of this section is to give you a deeper understanding of the concepts of metaprogramming by looking at each of the building blocks in detail, along with examples of how they can be applied in a practical scenario. This section will have chapters that follow an explanation of the concepts with an implementation-based approach to give hands-on experience along with guided coding knowledge to users while reading this book. The chapters in this section can be read sequentially or independently.

  • Chapter 4, Working with Metaclasses
  • Chapter 5, Understanding Introspection
  • Chapter 6, Implementing Reflection on Python Objects
  • Chapter 7, Understanding Generics and Typing
  • Chapter 8, Defining Templates for Algorithms
